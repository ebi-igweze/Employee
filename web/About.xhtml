About Employee Self Service

These Employee Self Service Features have been designed to act as an intermediary between Employees and the management.

The Employee Self Service is designed as an interface to give employees more control over their information by providing ready access to their profile and personal information, a view of their benefits; like amount of vacation days (leave), and it also has provisions to request IT assistance , contact Human Resources for vacation requests and apply for training Programs.

It also contains additional features that allow for viewing, editing and updating profile information, an embedded chatting application for communication amongst colleagues and a consistent access to news updates of the work environment.
